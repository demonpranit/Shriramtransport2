Join SkyNet Couriers: Franchise Opportunity

Become a SkyNet Couriers Zonal Franchise: India’s Fastest Expanding E-Com Courier Service

SkyNet Couriers invites motivated individuals to join our expanding team as Zonal Franchise. This is an exciting opportunity for you to book and deliver parcels, earning a steady income while representing one of India’s fastest-expanding e-commerce courier services. We are looking for passionate, results-driven individuals who can maintain the high service standards that SkyNet Couriers is known for.

Here’s an overview of what makes this franchise opportunity unique:

Pin Codes for Business:

As a Zonal Franchisee, you will be assigned 1 PIN codes covering a 1 km radius to manage, ensuring streamlined and efficient operations within a defined territory. If the number of PIN codes is fewer, the complete district.

Franchise Fee:

Secure your franchise with a refundable security deposit of Rs. 95,000/-, providing peace of mind and a safety net for both parties.

Parcel Volume:

Franchise can expect to deliver over 200+ parcels daily per pin code, ensuring a consistent and substantial volume of work.

Earning Per Parcel:

You will earn Rs. 48/- per parcel delivered, creating a direct correlation between your efforts and earnings.

Courier Booking Revenue:

Franchisees also receive 30% commission on each courier booking, providing an additional income stream.

Service Offering:

Your franchise will manage both courier bookings and parcel deliveries, covering the entire logistics cycle for customers and businesses alike.

Space Requirement:

A minimum of 100 to 150 sq. ft of office space is required to set up your franchise, providing a functional base for operations.

Contract Duration:

The franchise agreement is for 5 years, allowing ample time to build and grow your business.

Support from SkyNet:

Benefit from comprehensive support, including marketing, delivery kits, software, training, management, and logistical assistance.

Staffing Requirements:

To handle deliveries, each franchise will need to employ 2 delivery boys, ensuring timely service and operational efficiency.

Income Guarantee

Expect a minimum monthly income of Rs. 1.5 to 2 Lakh after expenses, derived from delivery fees, courier commissions, and performance bonuses.

Key Benefits

Financial Growth:

The pay-per-delivery model and courier booking commissions offer substantial income potential.

Training and Support:

SkyNet Couriers provides comprehensive training and continuous support to enhance your operational efficiency.

Brand Power:

Leverage the trusted SkyNet Couriers brand to attract customers and accelerate growth.

This is the ideal opportunity for entrepreneurs seeking a lucrative and sustainable business within the logistics industry, backed by a trusted and rapidly expanding brand. Join SkyNet Couriers as an Zonal Franchisee and build a successful future in India’s booming e-commerce logistics sector!
