Join SkyNet Couriers: Franchise Opportunity

Become a SkyNet Couriers Regional Franchise: India’s Fastest Expanding E-Com Courier Service

SkyNet Couriers invites motivated individuals to join our expanding team as Regional Franchisees. This is an exciting opportunity for you to book and deliver parcels, earning a steady income while representing one of India’s fastest-expanding e-commerce courier services. We are looking for passionate, results-driven individuals who can maintain the high service standards that SkyNet Couriers is known for.

Here’s an overview of what makes this franchise opportunity unique:

Pin Codes for Business:

As a Regional Franchise, you will be assigned a 10 pin codes and a 10 km radius to manage, ensuring a focused and manageable area for efficient operations or franchisee will be assigned full district if pin codes are less.

Franchise Fee:

Secure your franchise with a refundable security deposit of Rs. 3,00,000/-, providing peace of mind and a safety net for both parties.

Parcel Volume:

Franchisees can expect to deliver 200+ parcels daily per pin code, ensuring a consistent and substantial business flow. That is total parcel delivery count will be 2000+.

Earning Per Parcel:

Earn Rs. 48/- for each parcel delivered, creating a direct corellation between your efforts and earnings.

Courier Booking Revenue:

Franchisees also receive 30% commission on each courier booking, providing an additional income stream.

Services Offering:

Your franchise will manage both courier bookings and parcel deliveries, covering the entire logistics cycle for customers and businesses alike.

Space REquirements:

A minimum of 400 to 500 sq. ft of office space is required to set up your franchise, providing a functional base for operations.

Contract Duration:

The franchise agreement is for 5 years, allowing ample time to build and grow your business.

Support from SkyNet:

Benefit from comprehensive support, including marketing, delivery kits, software, training, management, and logistical assistance.

Staffing Requirements:

To handle deliveries, each franchise will need to employ 20 delivery boys, ensuring timely service and operational efficiency.

InCome Guarantee

Expect a minimum monthly income of Rs. 10-15 Lakh after expenses, derived from delivery fees, courier commissions, and performance bonuses.

Key Benefits

Financial Growth Opportunities:

With a pay-per-delivery compensation model and courier booking commissions, SkyNet Couriers offers significant income potential, enabling you to scale your earnings as your business grows.

Comprehensive Training and Ongoing Support:

SkyNet Couriers is committed to your success, offering detailed training and continuous support to ensure you optimize your operations and maximize efficiency.

Powerful Brand Recognition:

Benefit from the established and trusted SkyNet Couriers brand, which provides instant credibility and attracts a steady stream of customers, helping you build your business quickly.

This is a prime opportunity for motivated entrepreneurs eager to establish a profitable and long-lasting business in the logistics industry. As an Regional Franchisee, you’ll be supported by a strong, expanding brand in India’s rapidly growing e-commerce logistics market. Partner with SkyNet Couriers today and start building a prosperous future!
