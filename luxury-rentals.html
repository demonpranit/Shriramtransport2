Luxury Rentals

Shriram Transport Services has established a premium foothold in the luxury vehicle rental segment, delivering top-tier chauffeured transport solutions for corporate and high-end individual clients. Renowned for reliability, elegance, and discretion, the Luxury Rentals division of Shriram Transport Services caters to a discerning clientele seeking both comfort and class in their travel experience.

Our luxury fleet includes a curated selection of world-class vehicles such as the Mercedes-Benz S-Class, BMW 7 Series, Audi A8, Jaguar XJL, and Toyota Vellfire, each maintained to pristine standards and equipped with the latest amenities. These vehicles are ideal for executive transfers, VIP events, weddings, and international business delegations.

Leading multinational corporations (MNCs) across IT, consulting, finance, and pharmaceuticals—such as Accenture, Deloitte, Pfizer, and HSBC—trust Shriram Transport Services to provide consistent, high-quality transport solutions for their top executives and international guests. With professional, uniformed chauffeurs trained in customer service and route optimization, we ensure punctuality, privacy, and a seamless travel experience.

Shriram’s operations are supported by a centralized dispatch system, 24/7 customer assistance, and GPS-enabled fleet tracking, ensuring complete safety and accountability. Our commitment to excellence has positioned us as a trusted partner for luxury transportation across metropolitan cities and business hubs in India.

Whether it’s an airport transfer or a multi-day corporate tour, Shriram Transport Services stands synonymous with luxury, professionalism, and dependability in the rental car segment.
