Corporate Car Rentals

Shriram Transport Services is a trusted name in the Indian car rental industry, offering premium and reliable transportation solutions to multinational corporations (MNCs) across major cities. With a strong emphasis on safety, punctuality, and professional service, we cater to the dynamic mobility needs of global enterprises operating in India.

We specialize in providing customized, long-term and short-term car rental solutions, backed by a well-maintained fleet of luxury sedans, executive SUVs, and economy vehicles. Our corporate clientele includes top-tier international MNCs such as Accenture, IBM, Amazon, Google, Microsoft, Cognizant, Capgemini, HSBC, Standard Chartered, Siemens, Ericsson, Samsung, Bosch, Deloitte, and Oracle. These companies rely on us for seamless employee transportation, airport transfers, intercity travel, and executive mobility services.

Our professionally trained chauffeurs, 24/7 customer support, GPS-enabled tracking, and adherence to global safety and hygiene standards make us a preferred mobility partner for MNCs seeking consistent and high-quality ground transportation in India. Shriram Transport Services understands the expectations of international businesses and ensures services that reflect professionalism, efficiency, and discretion.

By combining decades of local expertise with a corporate service ethos, Shriram Transport Services stands as a dependable and scalable mobility partner for multinational companies aiming to offer their employees safe, comfortable, and timely transportation experiences. We continuously innovate to meet the evolving demands of corporate travel, making us a key enabler of business mobility across India.
