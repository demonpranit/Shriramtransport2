About Us

About SkyNet Couriers

SkyNet Couriers, a prominent player in the logistics industry, is committed to providing top-tier courier and delivery services both across India and internationally. Founded with a vision to redefine the logistics landscape, the company offers a wide array of solutions to cater to the unique requirements of businesses, individuals, and organizations alike.

Headquartered in Delhi, SkyNet Couriers has experienced rapid expansion since its establishment, growing into a network with more than 800 branches nationwide. Our continued success can be attributed to our unwavering commitment to delivering exceptional service, alongside our agility in adapting to the evolving needs of the market. With a focus on innovation, technological advancement, and operational efficiency, we have solidified our position as a trusted partner for businesses seeking timely and dependable delivery services.

SkyNet Couriers is officially registered under the name of SkyNet Couriers Private Limited, founded in 2020 by a dynamic and forward-thinking group of entrepreneurs: Binoy Choudhury, Raktim Kalita, Pappu Kumar, K Chhetri, and Ajay Kumar. Since its inception, the company has been dedicated to transforming the logistics industry through cutting-edge and effective courier solutions.
